The Platform

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A focused toolkit that covers the full improvement cycle — from first insight to live code.

🔄
Behavioural Feedback Loop
Savory captures micro-behaviours traditional analysts miss — mouse hesitation indicating confusion, scroll velocity revealing frustration, click patterns exposing decision friction, form abandonment surfacing broken flows. Every interaction is a data point. Every data point is an opportunity.
🎯
Interface Optimisation
AI-driven recommendations for your UI, layouts, and conversion paths — grounded entirely in how your actual users behave.
🗺️
User Flow Analysis
Deep navigation analysis that surfaces drop-offs, dead ends, and hidden friction before they cost you customers.
🧪
The Lab
An experimental space for testing design variations and new ideas before they go live to your full audience.
📡
Behavioural Data Capture
A lightweight Chrome extension that auto-captures clicks, screenshots, and navigation in real time — zero manual setup.
🤝
AI Sparring Partner
More than a dashboard — a knowledgeable collaborator that proposes solutions tailored to your product goals and industry.
